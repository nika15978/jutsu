water techniques

The element of Water (Suiton) is one of the basic natural types of chakra. Not every ninja is capable of creating large amounts of water from nothing. It is much easier to use ready-made water. The Element of Water is superior in strength to the Element of Fire and at the same time inferior in strength to the Element of Earth. Water is a component of the Kekkei Genkai techniques - Ice (Hyoton), Steam (Futton), Storm (Ranton) and Wood (Mokuton).

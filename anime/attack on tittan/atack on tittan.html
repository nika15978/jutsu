Genres:adventures,action movie,drama,fantasy and thriller.

Subject:military,shonen andsuperpower,

Release years:2013,2017,2018,2020

Original name: Shingeki no Kyojin

Cornered humanity is living its last days under the yoke of the titans - mysterious creatures that terrorize the human race. The survivors huddle behind the walls of a large settlement with their own government, representing the last stronghold of centralized power in the world. A young man named Eren also lives there, spending his childhood in relative safety, until an out of the ordinary event occurs: the titans break into the city, destroying everything in their path. After the invasion of the giants, Eren becomes an orphan and vows to destroy every single titan. On the way to his goal, he and his friends join a reconnaissance squad engaged in sorties over the wall. The hero does not yet know how many mysteries the outside world is fraught with.
The adaptation of the manga about the adventures of Eren and his comrades Shingeki no Kyojin "blew up" the global anime community in 2013 and attracted many fans of Japanese animation.

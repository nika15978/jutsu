Genres:comedy and Romance

Subject:school

Release years:2017,2022

Original name: Youkoso Jitsuryoku Shijou Shugi no Kyoushitsu e

Kodo Ikusei High School is a prestigious educational institution with very good performance, where every student after graduation is guaranteed to enter the most prestigious institute and be able to find a job. Students are allowed to walk as they wish: with any hairstyle, in any form, bring their personal belongings with them. Upon admission, each student is awarded 100 points (100,000 yen), on which they are required to live. Points are awarded every month. At first glance, Kodo Ikusei is a paradise for any student, but it was not so simple. Points are awarded for academic performance, and depending on the rank of the class. The school has a so-called “es-system”, according to which classes are distributed into ranks, according to the type: D, C, B, A Kiyotaki Ayanokōji is the protagonist who was assigned to Class D, a class where the school's administration dumps students with low abilities. In order to move to a higher rank, a class needs to score more points on the test than the class that is at a higher rank, in which case the class that surpassed the other will take its place, and the other class will go down. After meeting Suzune Horikita and Kikyo Kushida, his position begins to change. Whether our hero and his classmates will be able to rise higher - we will see when viewing.

Spatiotemporal Techniques

Senjutsu is a type of technique used with the help of natural chakra instead of the basic one. In the process of accumulating natural chakra, a transformation occurs, more commonly known as the Hermit Mode. The Hermit Technique is a form of Senjutsu. It can be both improved techniques and completely new ones.
